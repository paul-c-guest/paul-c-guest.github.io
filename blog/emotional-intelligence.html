Emotional Intelligence

26 June 2023

What is Emotional Intelligence?

In my own words: it is an ability to recognise emotional states in yourself and in others, and to act and react in a constructive manner.

How is EQ different to IQ?

Well: if IQ is how good you are at understanding and solving problems that are best suited to your logical or rational side, then EQ is something akin to your ability to do the same for emotional problems.

Why is EQ important?

As people in societies, we are not only presented with technical, logical problems from day to day. The problems we face each day will likely hold some emotional aspect, whether those emotions are our own, someone elses, or both; and can come as part of a technical problem at the same time.

Being capable of working through all kinds of challenges, towards constructive outcomes, is surely better for everyone.
